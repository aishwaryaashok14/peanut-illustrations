# Code of Conduct

Peanut Illustrations is a small creative tool. Please keep discussion kind, specific, and useful.

Expected behavior:

- be respectful of different levels of design and technical experience
- critique the work, not the person
- explain visual or technical feedback clearly
- give attribution when building on someone else's idea

Not welcome:

- harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- discriminatory language or behavior
- spam, self-promotion without context, or bad-faith argument
- sharing private information without permission

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block contributors who make the project unpleasant or unsafe for others.
